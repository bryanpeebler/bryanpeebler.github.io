Our Programs: Community Potluck

Community Potluck events are held on the first Thursday of the month—during the school year in the multipurpose room at Theodore Judah Elementary (101 Dean Way in Folsom).

This is a fun and relaxed way to get to know the volunteers, staff and families involved in Folsom’s Hope programs. Bring your family, bring a dish to share, and be ready to have fun!

Please note that children must attend with a parent or guardian.

Our Potluck schedule and related announcements are posted on our Facebook page. Feel free to contact Salwa Kasabian for more details. We hope to see you there!

Meeting the Community We Serve

Our community potlucks are one of my favorite Folsom's Hope events. It is always wonderful to meet the families we serve, and to make connections that help us serve those families better. We can't wait to meet you!

-- Debi Kauffman, Folsom's Hope Board Member
