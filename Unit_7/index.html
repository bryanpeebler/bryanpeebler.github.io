Our Programs

Mentoring

JUMP (Judah United Mentor Project), BAM (Bears and Mentors) and SMAK (Sutter Mentors and Kids) are mentoring programs where at-risk children connect with a caring, supportive adult once a week during their school-scheduled lunch hour.

Learn More

Tutoring

Folsom's Hope offers two tutoring programs: STARs (Students Together Achieving Results) -- an after-school program for Grades 3-5, and Peer Tutoring, which matches students with tutors from Folsom High and Folsom Lake college.

Learn More

Community Events

Community Potluck events offer a fun and relaxed way to get to know the volunteers, staff and families involved in Folsom’s Hope programs. Bring your family, bring a dish to share, and be ready to have fun!

A Place for Hope: Folsom's Hope Community Center

Folsom's Hope has taken on the goal of building a family resource center on land just adjacent to Theodore Judah Elementary School. The center will provide a central place to serve the needs of Folsom's at-risk children and their families. Find out more about this ambitious project and how you can help!
