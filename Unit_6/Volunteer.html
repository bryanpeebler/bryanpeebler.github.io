Volunteer Opportunities: The Gift of Time

Volunteering is an amazing way to help the children served by Folsom's Hope! It doesn't take much -- just an hour or so a week -- to change the life of an at-risk child. There are several ways to help out, including:

  • 1:1 mentoring
  • After-school tutoring and homework help
  • Providing in-class help at Folsom Lake High School
  • Becoming part of the Pray Team
