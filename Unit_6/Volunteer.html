Volunteer Opportunities: The Gift of Time

Volunteering is an amazing way to help the children served by Folsom's Hope! It doesn't take much -- just an hour or so a week -- to change the life of an at-risk child.

There are several ways you can help, including:

  • 1:1 mentoring during elementary and middle school lunchtimes
  • After-school tutoring and homework help
  • Providing in-class help at Folsom Lake High School
  • Joining the Pray Team, on most Mondays, to pray for our schools and the surrounding community

If you'd like to learn more about volunteer opportunities, contact Salwa Kasabian. We are happy to help guide you to the right opportunity!


Note: Some volunteer opportunities require fingerprinting and a TB test. The Folsom's Hope team can help facilitate this process.

I've had the opportunity to work with Folsom's Hope kids for several years now, in both the mentoring and tutoring programs. Seeing how these kids progress with just a little bit of love and help has been amazing. I've learned as much from the kids as they've learned from me!!!
