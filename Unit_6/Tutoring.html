Our Programs: Tutoring

We believe in the life-changing power of one-on-one teaching and coaching. Struggling students receive both academic help and loving encouragement to keep learning.

Folsom's Hope offers two different tutoring programs:

STARS (Students Together Achieving Results)

STARS is an after-school program for grades 3-5 at Theodore Judah Elementary and Blanche Sprentz Elementary schools. In addition to help with homework and a healthy snack, kids enjoy recreational activity, exposure to the arts, and a fun, welcoming environment where they can form friendships. STARS also offers a safe, loving place for kids to be between 3-6PM, a time when many of them would otherwise be home alone.

Peer Tutoring

Students from Folsom High School and Folsom's Lake college provide tutoring and literacy support for middle and elementary school students.

If you'd like to learn more about tutoring opportunities, contact Salwa Kasabian. We'd love to match you with the right opportunity!

A Volunteer's Perspective

I've had the opportunity to work with Folsom's Hope kids for several years now, in both the mentoring and tutoring programs. Seeing how these kids progress with just a little bit of love and help has been amazing. I've learned as much from the kids as they've learned from me!!!

-- Sonja S., Folsom's Hope Volunteer
